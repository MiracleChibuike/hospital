About the Neurology

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders. Our team of
neurologists uses cutting-edge technology to provide comprehensive care for conditions affecting the brain and
nervous system.

Neurological rehabilitation programs

Advanced neuroimaging (e.g., MRI, CT scans)

Comprehensive neurological evaluations

Treatment of neurological disorders

About the Diagnostics

The Diagnostics Department focuses on identifying and diagnosing a wide range of health conditions using

Comprehensive lab tests

Imaging services (e.g., X-rays, MRIs, CT scans)

Blood tests and analyses

Genetic testing and screening

About the Dental

Our Dental Department focuses on maintaining and improving oral health using advanced techniques
and technology. Our dental specialists are experts in their field, providing comprehensive care for optimal dental health.

Routine dental check-ups and cleanings

Cosmetic dentistry (e.g., teeth whitening, veneers)

Orthodontic services (e.g., braces, Invisalign)

Restorative treatments (e.g., fillings, crowns, bridges)

About the Ophthalmology

Our Ophthalmology Department focuses on maintaining and improving eye health using advanced
techniques and technology. Our ophthalmologists are experts in their field, providing comprehensive care
for optimal vision and eye health.

Comprehensive eye exams

Cataract surgery

Treatment for glaucoma and macular degeneration

Corrective procedures (e.g., LASIK, PRK)

About the Emergency

The Emergency Department is dedicated to providing immediate and life-saving care for a wide range of
medical emergencies. Our emergency physicians and nurses are experts in their field, ensuring prompt and
effective treatment for all patients. We are equipped with the latest technology and resources to handle
critical situations and provide comprehensive emergency care.

24/7 emergency care

Trauma care and stabilization

Acute medical treatment (e.g., heart attacks, strokes)

Rapid diagnostic services (e.g., imaging, lab tests)

About the Oncology

The Oncology Department is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for patients
with cancer. Our oncologists and specialized staff are experts in diagnosing and treating various types of
cancer, ensuring personalized treatment plans for each patient.

Comprehensive cancer screenings and diagnostics

Chemotherapy and radiation therapy

Surgical oncology procedures

Personalized treatment plans and targeted therapies
